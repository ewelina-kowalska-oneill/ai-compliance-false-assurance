# Case Study: AI-Driven Compliance and False Assurance

## Scenario
A fast-growing SaaS startup adopts an AI-driven compliance platform to accelerate SOC 2 certification.

Within weeks, the company achieves certification and begins onboarding enterprise customers.

## Hidden Reality
- Policies generated automatically
- Risk registers pre-filled
- Control evidence templated
- Minimal manual validation

The audit is conducted by a low-rigor firm relying heavily on provided documentation.

## Incident
Six months later:
- A data exposure incident occurs
- Logs are incomplete
- Access reviews were never performed
- Incident response procedures were not tested

## Outcome
The enterprise customer discovers:
- Controls existed only on paper
- Certification did not reflect reality
- Vendor risk was underestimated

## Key Issue
False assurance created by:
- Over-reliance on certification
- Lack of control validation
- Automation without governance
