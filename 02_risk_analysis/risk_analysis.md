# Risk Analysis

## Overview
This scenario highlights systemic third-party risk failures driven by over-reliance on compliance certifications.

## Key Risks Identified

### 1. False Assurance Risk
Organisations assume compliance certification equals security.

### 2. AI-Generated Evidence Risk
Automated compliance tools may generate misleading or incomplete evidence.

### 3. Auditor Reliability Risk
Audit quality varies significantly across providers.

### 4. Control Effectiveness Risk
Controls may be documented but not operating.

## Risk Themes
- Trust without verification
- Speed over assurance
- Automation without accountability
